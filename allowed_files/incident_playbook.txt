ACME Corp Security Incident Response Playbook — v1.4

SEVERITY LEVELS
P1 — Critical: Active breach, data exfiltration, ransomware. Immediate response.
P2 — High: Suspected compromise, unauthorized access attempt. 2-hour SLA.
P3 — Medium: Policy violation, phishing attempt (no click). 24-hour SLA.
P4 — Low: Suspicious but unconfirmed activity. 72-hour SLA.

P1 RESPONSE STEPS
1. Notify security@acme.com AND call the 24/7 hotline: ext. 4911
2. Isolate affected systems from the network (do not power off)
3. Preserve all logs — do not clear, rotate, or delete
4. Do not communicate details over potentially compromised channels
5. Engage legal counsel if PII or regulated data is involved
6. Document all actions taken with timestamps

PHISHING RESPONSE
1. Do not click links or open attachments
2. Forward the email as an attachment to phishing@acme.com
3. Delete from inbox after forwarding
4. If you clicked a link: treat as P2 — report immediately

POST-INCIDENT
Root cause analysis required for all P1 and P2 incidents.
RCA document due within 5 business days of resolution.
Lessons learned reviewed in monthly security meeting.
