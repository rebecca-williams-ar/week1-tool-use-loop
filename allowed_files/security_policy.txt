ACME Corp Information Security Policy — v2.1

1. ACCESS CONTROL
   All systems require MFA. Shared credentials are prohibited.
   Access is granted on least-privilege basis and reviewed quarterly.

2. DATA CLASSIFICATION
   Confidential: PII, financial records, API keys, credentials.
   Internal: Business processes, employee data, product roadmaps.
   Public: Marketing materials, published documentation.

3. INCIDENT RESPONSE
   Report suspected breaches to security@acme.com within 1 hour.
   Do not attempt self-remediation on compromised systems.
   Preserve logs — do not power off affected machines.

4. ACCEPTABLE USE
   Company systems are for business purposes only.
   Personal cloud storage (Dropbox, personal Google Drive) is prohibited
   for storing company data.

5. API AND CREDENTIAL MANAGEMENT
   All API keys stored in approved secret management systems only.
   Keys rotated every 90 days or immediately upon suspected exposure.
   No credentials in source code, ever.
