ACME Corp New Employee Onboarding Guide

WEEK 1 CHECKLIST
- Complete I-9 and direct deposit forms with HR (submit by day 2)
- Enroll in MFA for all company accounts (required before system access)
- Complete Security Awareness Training in the LMS (due by end of week 1)
- Request access to required systems via the IT Service Desk ticket portal
- Meet with your manager to review your 30/60/90 day plan

IT SETUP
Your laptop will be provisioned by IT on day 1.
Do not install software without an approved IT Service Desk ticket.
VPN is required for all remote access to internal systems.

BENEFITS ENROLLMENT
Open enrollment window: first 30 days of employment.
Health, dental, vision, and 401k elections made in Workday.
Contact benefits@acme.com with questions.
